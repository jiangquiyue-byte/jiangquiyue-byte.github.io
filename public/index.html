TCM Fundamentals

Understanding the foundations.

Traditional Chinese Medicine is built on centuries of observation and practice. Learn the core concepts that guide herbal prescriptions and acupressure treatments.

☯️

Yin & Yang

The fundamental duality of the universe. Health is maintained when yin and yang are in balance within the body.

🌀

Qi (Vital Energy)

The life force that flows through all living things. When Qi flows freely, the body is healthy. When blocked, illness occurs.

🔥

Five Elements

Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water — each element corresponds to organs, emotions, and seasons in the body.

🫀

Meridian System

The network of energy pathways connecting the body. Acupressure points lie along these meridians.
